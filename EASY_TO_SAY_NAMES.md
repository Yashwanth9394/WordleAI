Try saying these out loud right now:

### ❌ FAILS THE TEST

- ❌ People will say "Quiv-ee-YO" or "KEE-vee-oh" (confusion)

- Say it 10 times: "Hintivo, Hintivo, Hintivo..."
- ❌ Three syllables, too long, sounds corporate

**"Braindash"** - BRAIN-dash
- Say it 10 times: "Braindash, Braindash..."
- ❌ Two hard consonants clash ("nd" then "d")
- ❌ Not smooth to say quickly

## 🎤 THE PHONETIC PLEASURE TEST

### What makes a word SATISFYING to say?

**1. Balanced syllables (1-2 max)**
- ✅ Zippy (2)
- ✅ Zenko (2)
- ✅ Quip (1)
- ✅ Movo (2)
- ❌ Quivio (3 - too long)

**2. Pleasant sound combinations**
- ✅ "Z" + soft vowel (Zippy, Zenko)
- ✅ "M" + soft vowel (Movo)
- ✅ "Qu" + short vowel (Quip)
- ❌ Hard consonant clusters

**3. Ends with vowel sound or soft consonant**
- ✅ Zippy (-ee)
- ✅ Zenko (-o)
- ✅ Movo (-o)
- ✅ Quip (soft -p)

**4. No pronunciation confusion**
- ✅ All of the above
- ❌ Quivio (3 different pronunciations possible)
