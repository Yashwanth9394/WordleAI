## 🛡️ The Smart Founder's Domain Strategy

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Your stage now)

**Must have:**
- ✅ ONE primary domain (.com, .game, .app, or .io)
- ✅ App store names available (iOS + Android)
- ✅ @username on major social (Twitter, Instagram, TikTok)

**DON'T waste money on:**
- ❌ Every TLD variation (.net, .org, .info, .biz)
- ❌ Typo domains (people use search, not typing URLs)
- ❌ Country TLDs you're not targeting

**Cost:** $10-50 for domain + $0 for social handles = **Total: ~$50**

---

### Phase 2: After Launch (0-100K users)

**Priority:**
1. **Trademark registration** ($250-350) ⭐ THIS IS YOUR REAL PROTECTION
2. Monitor and buy ONLY critical variants:
   - Typosquatting of your EXACT name (if it's getting traffic)
   - Maybe .app if you launched with .com

**Don't buy:**
- ❌ Every possible variation
- ❌ Defensive domains "just in case"

**Why:** Your $500 is better spent on marketing than domains nobody visits

---

### Phase 3: Growth Stage (100K-1M users)

**Now you have money and brand value:**
- Buy the .com if you don't have it
- Buy major typo variations if they're stealing traffic
- Protect your trademark aggressively

**Why it matters now:**
- You have revenue to afford it
- Brand confusion actually costs you users
- Domain prices will be higher, but you can afford it

## 🎯 The Real Protection: TRADEMARK

### Why Trademark > Domain Portfolio

**Scenario without trademark:**
- You own: hintly.com
- Squatter buys: hintly.net, hintly.io, hintly.app
- Squatter redirects traffic to ads or competitor
- You can't do anything (they legally own those domains)

**Scenario WITH trademark:**
- You own: hintly.com
- You trademarked: "HINTLY" for word games
- Squatter buys: hintly.net and tries to profit
- You file UDRP complaint (Uniform Domain-Dispute Resolution Policy)
- You WIN and take the domain for ~$1,500 legal cost
- Squatter is forced to transfer domain to you

**Real example:** Facebook filed 200+ UDRP complaints and won 95% of them.

---

## 📋 UDRP Process (Your Legal Weapon)

### What is UDRP?

**ICANN policy that lets trademark owners take domains from squatters**

**You can win if you prove:**
1. ✅ Domain is identical/confusingly similar to YOUR trademark
2. ✅ Squatter has NO legitimate interest in the domain
3. ✅ Domain was registered in BAD FAITH (to profit from your brand)

**Cost:** $1,500 filing fee + $1,000 lawyer (optional)
**Timeline:** 60-90 days
**Success rate:** 85%+ if you have strong trademark

## 🏆 The Actual Strategy Successful Founders Use

### Step 1: Secure the Essentials ($50)
- Primary domain (.com or .game or .app)
- Social handles (@name on Twitter, Instagram, TikTok)

### Step 2: File Trademark ($350)
- File USPTO trademark application
- This is your REAL protection
- Allows you to take domains later via UDRP

### Step 3: Launch & Build ($0 defensive spending)
- Don't waste money on domain variations
- Focus 100% on product and marketing
- Let squatters buy variations (they're wasting THEIR money)

### Step 4: Monitor & React (Post-launch)
- Use Google Alerts for your brand name
- If someone builds actual competing site, take action
- If it's just parked domain, ignore it

### Step 5: Buy Important Domains When You Have Revenue
- After 10K users: Buy .app or .io if you don't have it
- After 100K users: Buy major typo domains
- After 1M users: Buy country TLDs you're expanding to
