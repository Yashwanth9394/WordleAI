# Names That Sound GRAND, RICH, and ASPIRATIONAL

## 🎩 The Psychology of "Wealthy" Sounding Names

### What makes a name sound grand?

**McDonald's** - The "Mc" prefix = heritage, family legacy
**Starbucks** - "Star" + nautical reference = adventure, prestige
**Mercedes** - Foreign, sophisticated, European elegance
**Rolex** - Strong consonants, premium sound
**Tiffany** - Feminine, elegant, luxury
**Cartier** - French, sophisticated, high-class

### Common patterns in "grand" names:

1. **Foreign/European sounds** (sophistication)
2. **Multiple syllables** (not rushed, leisurely)
3. **Soft consonants + vowels** (elegant flow)
4. **Classical/Latin roots** (timeless, educated)
5. **Open vowel endings** (-a, -o, -e) (expansive feeling)
